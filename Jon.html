YMCA University of Science and Technology(YMCA UST) is a state university located in Faridabad, in the state of Haryana, India. The YMCA University of Science and Technology, erstwhile YMCA Institute of Engineering, was established in the year 1969, as a joint venture of the National Council of YMCAs of India, Govt of Haryana, and the Central Agencies for Development Aid, Bonn, Germany. It started as an Indo-German project with an aim to impart technical education to a developing India on German pattern. The labs & workshops have been setup with the assistance of German expertise. In 1996, State Govt. of Haryana took complete control of the Institute and upgraded it to University status in December 2009.

Campus

The campus of the YMCA University of Science and Technology is located in the sprawling Faridabad-Ballabgarh Industrial complex, on NH-2 about 30 miles from the Indian capital New Delhi. The nearest metro station (on about a walking distance of five minutes) is Escorts Mujesar on the Violet Line of DMRC. The Escorts Mujesar - Ballabhgarh stretch of Violet Line will open by the end of 2018. The Campus has three main buildings, one for MBA and the remaining two for other degree courses. There is a canteen, an eco-café, a stationery shop and a Mother Dairy open during university hours. For the students into sports, there is a Basketball Court, a Badminton Court, a Football/Cricket ground, Archery Area. Free Dispensary is also there which opens during university hours.

Student life

There are various cultural as well as technical clubs/societies of which the students can be a part of. The cultural clubs in YMCA are :

  • Srijan - Quest Never Ends - Fine Arts and Crafts Club
  • Vividha - Dramatics Society
  • Nataraja - Western/Bhangra Dance Club
  • Tarannum - Music Society
  • Jhalak - Photography Club
  • Vivekanand Manch
  • Ananya - Debating and Literature Society

The technical clubs in YMCA are :

  • Microbird
  • Samarpan
  • Mechnext
  • Manan
  • BAHA SAE
